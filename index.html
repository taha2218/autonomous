Car Modules

An autonomous car is a vehicle that is capable of driving itself with minimum or no human involvement by sensing and understanding its environment. Self Driving Cars involve the use of sensors, actuators, complex algorithms, AI systems and powerful processors to execute software. Object Detection in autonomous vehicles is used to help understand and perceive the surroundings of the vehicle for its safe driving. These are few models that apply deep learning and mathemadical models for the functioning of autnomous cars.

MOTIVATION

A range of potential benefits that could revolutionize driving for the better led to the idea of building autonomous vehicles. These are greater road safety, greater independence, more economical and more productivity.

OBJECTIVES

  • 1. To see the environment and navigate to the destination without harm
  • 2. To detect & identify/classify obstacles around the vehicle and make real-time decisions based on that
  • 3. To predict accurate steering angles by taking input of the lane
  • 4. To accurately detect lanes
  • 5. Sentiment Analysis of the drivers audio in order to predict human behaviour and contact respective authorities if need be.
